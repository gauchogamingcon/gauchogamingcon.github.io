About Gaucho Gaming
Gaucho Gaming is University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB)’s student-run gaming organization dedicated to building community through games, events, and creative collaboration. From tournaments, giveaways, and social nights to large-scale productions like GG Con, we bring students together through a shared love of gaming culture.
