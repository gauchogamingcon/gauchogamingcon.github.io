Meet Patrick Pedraza at GGCon. They will be participating in... "From Mondstadt to Inazuma: Genshin Impact Voice Actor Panel," for his role as Wanderer!

<div class = break></div>

Hailing from Oxnard, California, Patrick Pedraza is a Latino Voice Actor currently best known for voicing the highly anticipated character Scaramouche on the game Genshin Impact. Patrick considers himself a versatile VA as he's been the voice of Good Luck Bear on the Care Bears series since 2018 and has voiced other characters such as Yun on Crunchyroll's Onyx Equinox, Sandy and Gus on Brawlstars and, Carlos Kitbash from Rescue Heroes. Patrick grew up in the punk scene and was a drummer in many bands that toured across the country. He still writes songs in his dad punk band "Dad Brains" but Voice Acting is his main passion.
