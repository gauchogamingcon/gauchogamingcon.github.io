Meet Nick Apostolides at GGCon. They will be participating in... "Voice Your Fears: A Talk with Nick Apostolides and Caleb Yen," for his role as Leon Kennedy (Resident Evil)!

<div class = break></div>

Nick Apostolides is best known for portraying the beloved character Leon S. Kennedy across multiple Resident Evil projects, including Capcom's RESIDENT EVIL 2 & 4 Remakes - both Game of the Year Nominees - as well as the animated NETFLIX series INFINITE DARKNESS.

In 2023, Nick received IGN's Horror Fest Award for Best Performance of the Year for his work in the RESIDENT EVIL 4 Remake. Most recently, Nick reprised the role of Leon in the latest franchise installment, RESIDENT EVIL 9: REQUIEM, one of the biggest entries of the series to date.

He has delivered voice, performance capture, and stunt work in other notable franchises, including OUTERWORLDS, DEADSPACE, WARFRAME 1999, BATTLEFIELD, APEX LEGENDS, CALL of DUTY, LIFE is STRANGE, MADDEN NFL, and MEDAL of HONOR. While he's most known for his work in video games, Nick has worked on over 100 commercials and was nominated for a New England Chapter Emmy for hosting the history show, AMERICAN RUINS.

In 2025, Nick was also featured in Entertainment Weekly for his participation in the Pixel Pack, a collaborative group of actors working to shape the future of the games industry, advocating for performers' rights, recognition, and protections against AI. Beyond Entertainment, Nick is deeply committed to humanitarian work. As host and master-builder on THE FIXERS for three seasons, he traveled the globe supporting incredible organizations to rebuild communities in 23 different countries.
