Meet Cory Yee at GGCon. They will be participating in... "From Mondstadt to Inazuma: Genshin Impact Voice Actor Panel", for his role as Gorou!

<div class = break></div>

Cory Yee is a Chinese American voice actor, most well known for Gorou in Genshin Impact, Akito in Ghostwire: Tokyo, Sigma and Bram Stoker in Bungo Stray Dogs, and The Beholder in the award winning To Your Eternity. He loves bringing characters to life, always looking for ways to make his soul and his clients happy!

Before becoming a voice actor, Cory worked as a production sound mixer, editor, and pretty much every other job under the sun for live action film. He also enjoys streaming, playing (way too much) video games, and photography.
