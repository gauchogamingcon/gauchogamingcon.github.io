<div class = break></div>

James Mathis III is an accomplished American actor and voice performer known for his commanding presence and dynamic range across film, television, and video games. He is especially recognized for his contributions to the Black Panther legacy, helping bring the world of Wakanda to life through powerful performances that have resonated with global audiences. With work spanning major franchises for Marvel, DC, and Disney, Mathis continues to deliver bold, memorable characters both on screen and behind the mic.
