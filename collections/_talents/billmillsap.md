Bill Millsap is a dynamic actor and director known for his commanding on-screen presence and grounded, intense performances. He is best recognized for portraying The Punisher, bringing a gritty realism and raw edge to the iconic antihero. Expanding his impact behind the camera, Millsap has also directed projects that showcase his strong storytelling instincts. He continues to make his mark across film and television both in front of and behind the lens.
