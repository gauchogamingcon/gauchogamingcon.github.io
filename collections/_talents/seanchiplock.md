Meet Sean Chiplock at GGCon. They will be participating in... "From Mondstadt to Inazuma: Genshin Impact Voice Actor Panel," for his role as Diluc!

<div class = break></div>

Possessing a huge performance arc from humans to creatures, Sean Chiplock is a sought-after performer bringing an authentic level of excitement and focus to his voice over roles. With a vocal range that goes from deep and menacing to a textured adult to even an innocent teen, his ability to adapt quickly to distinctive genres from videogames to anime, features, and name-brand commercials has helped him surprise clients and audiences alike with his utility. Just a few of his most well-known roles are Revali, Teba, and The Great Deku Tree in the "Legend of Zelda" franchise; Diluc Ragnvindr in "Genshin Impact"; Rean Schwarzer in the series of games "Legend of Heroes"; Ky Kiske in "Guilty Gear Strive"; Noob Saibot in "Mortal Kombat"; and Nero in the "Final Fantasy VII" remakes.

The same passion that has helped Sean excel in his career growth and performances has also made him a tour-de-force with fans and special events. His enthusiasm for animals, food, gaming, storytelling, and comedy shines through in his interactions, and his ability to create memorable moments with community members has made him a high-demand favorite for conventions across the globe. Sean enjoys building bridges between the industry and his audience in his Twitch livestreams, where he encourages others to explore their own strengths with the goal of fostering an ever-stronger industry of talented veterans.
