<div class = break></div>

Jon Bailey is a powerhouse voice actor best known as the iconic narrator of Honest Trailers, where his bold, cinematic delivery has defined the series for millions of fans. He has also brought fan-favorite characters to life, including Jeff the Landshark from Marvel, and legendary voices like Soundwave and Shockwave from Transformers. Known for his versatility and unmistakable tone, Bailey continues to deliver unforgettable performances across animation, games, and digital media.
