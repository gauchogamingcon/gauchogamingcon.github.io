# What is GG Con?
GG Con 2026 is an all day convention of gaming and community hosted by Gaucho Gaming, happening Saturday, May 23rd, 2026.

<div class = break></div>

Join us for tournaments, voice actor panels, VTubers, workshops, art alley, and collaborations with student clubs across campus. Whether you’re here to compete, learn, create, or just hang out, GG Con has something for everyone.
