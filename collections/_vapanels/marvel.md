Bill is known for his commanding, gritty vocal performances across games and animation, bringing intensity and realism to the Punisher.

A veteran voice actor in the gaming industry, James has portrayed Black Panther across multiple titles, delivering a powerful and regal performance beloved by fans.

Xanthe’s versatile voice work spans anime and video games, and her portrayal of Dagger showcases her ability to balance strength with compassion.
